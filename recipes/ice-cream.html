Ice Cream

Description

Here's how to make a bouquet of ice cream, if you'd ever want such a thing.

Ingredients

  • ice cream of many colors
  • waffle cone
  • glue

Instructions

  1. Glue many ice cream scoops together.
  2. Glue the mass of ice cream scoops to the waffle cone.
  3. Serve chilled with knife and fork.
