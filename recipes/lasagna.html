Lasagna

Description

This is my personal favorite lasagna recipe, adapted from J Kenji's, which I found to be delicious but too rich.

Ingredients

  • lasagna noodles
  • tomato sauce
  • 400 pounds of ground pork
  • 200 pounds of ground lamb
  • 1 ton of mozzerella cheese, hand shredded

Instructions

  1. Raise one litter of pigs until they are 3 years old.
  2. Slaughter them all at the same time before they can understand what's happening.
  3. Grind them up in a meat grinder, all parts, including skin.
  4. Spread on parcooked lasagna noodles.
  5. Cover in sauce and cheese.
  6. Bake for 14 hours at 225 degrees F. No preheating of oven required.
  7. Enjoy with 40 of your closest frirends.
