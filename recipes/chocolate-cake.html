Chocolate cake

Description

This is a recipe for chocolate cake that I got from my friend Charlie.

Ingredients

  • flour
  • unsweetened baking chocolate
  • 3 eggs
  • 4 cups sugar
  • 1 stick butter

Instructions

  1. Mix all wet ingredients.
  2. Mix all dry ingredients.
  3. Mix wet and dry ingredients together.
  4. Pour everything into greased baking pan.
  5. Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes.
  6. Perform toothpick test to see if fully cooked. If not cooked, cook longer.
  7. Eat in one sitting.
